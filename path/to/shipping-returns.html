Shipping & Returns

Learn about our shipping and return policies

What are your shipping options?
We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping delivers within 1-3 business days.
How much does shipping cost?
Shipping costs vary based on the delivery location and the shipping method selected. You can view the exact shipping cost during checkout.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999.
Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the courier’s platform.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it in its original condition for a refund or exchange.
How do I initiate a return?
To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at support@giftaura.com with your order details. We will guide you through the process.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive the returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
